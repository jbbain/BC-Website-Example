The Morton and Angela Topfer Library Café is CUNY's only 24/7 computing facility. Originally based on the concept of a downtown internet cafe, the Library Café has grown into a full service computing facility, with more than 80 workstations, all with internet access and a legion of software applications.

The Library Café also offers full printing and scanning services, multiple study, dining and lounge areas, expert and caring technical support, and a full service Starbucks Coffee kiosk!

The Library Café staff also offers a year- round program of free computer workshops. These famous and well regarded classes are held throughout the week, for absolute beginners and experienced users alike, and cover a wide range of computer- based topics.
